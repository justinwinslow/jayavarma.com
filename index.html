Jaya Varma

Hello!

My name is Jaya Varma and I am a violinist currently working in the greater Miami area.

After receiving my Master's degree from the University of Michigan where I studied with the amazing Yehonatan Berick, I spent two years as a freelance violinist and educator in Boston. During that time I served as Resident Artist for the acclaimed el sistema program at the Conservatory Lab Charter School in Brighton, MA. If you are at all interested in the arts in schools you need to check out this school! The program is one of a kind and I will never forget the beautiful kids and teachers I worked with there. Additionally, I taught Suzuki violin at the All Newton Music School. As a performer, I served as concertmaster for the Brookline Symphony and played regularly with the world class chamber orchestra, Discovery Ensemble.

If you are looking for strings for your Miami-area wedding or event, please visit the section on weddings and events. I know you will find the information helpful as you put together the details for your special occasion!

You can also get in touch with me any time using the contact form at the bottom of the page.

Teaching

Teaching in Boston really gave me some perspective on the state of music education and what it takes to mold an energetic kid into a musician. The experience has made me an outstanding group and private teacher.

There are many pedagogical ideas and methods out there. While I primarily use the Suzuki method for beginners, I firmly believe in the development of a complete musician. This involves incorporating ideas and exercises from a variety of sources depending on the needs of the individual.

It's never too late to start playing a string instrument! I have experience teaching students from ages 3-65 and have enjoyed the unique challenges teaching provides.
